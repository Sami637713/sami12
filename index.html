About Pakistan Air Force

The Pakistan Air Force (PAF) is the aerial warfare branch of the Pakistan Armed Forces, primarily tasked with the aerial defense of Pakistan with a secondary role of providing air support to the Pakistan Army and Navy.

Established on 14 August 1947, the PAF has participated in various conflicts including the Indo-Pakistani wars of 1965 and 1971, and has been involved in anti-terrorism operations.

The PAF operates a diverse fleet of aircraft including JF-17 Thunder (jointly developed with China), F-16 Fighting Falcons, and Mirage fighter jets.
